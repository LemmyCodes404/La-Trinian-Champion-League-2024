Rules and Guidelines

If rules are broken, it will lead to disqualification

No usage of Third Party Apps

Any type of exploitation will result in a disqualification of the member/whole team

Trash Talking is NOT Tolerated

Any forms of offensive gesture to teamates and rivals will result in penalty points to the whole team. Be respectful.

Missing Activities are NOT allowed

Please finish your Missing Activities before joining LTCL. Students with missing activities shall pass their activities before joining

No Account Sharing

Sharing to a person of higher rank than the rank intended will have a punishment judeged by the Marshals and Supervisors

Must be an LTA student from Grade 6 or Above

This is to ensure fair play to all participants.

Must have a Discord Account

Before joining, make sure that you have a discord account. Students under the age of 13 shall have their guardian create their Discord Account.
